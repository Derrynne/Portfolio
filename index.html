Hi, I’m a Graphic Designer & Illustrator based in Varna, Bulgaria!

Below you can find some of my projects.

Calendar Design

Stylized calendar completed as a course project for my AI course. The design is cohesive and vibrant. For each three months there is a different color scheme, corresponding to the season.

Odesos Brand Design

This brand design includes several items. It was commissioned by a dairy company but unfortunately the products never reached the market. It includes logo, stamp, label, cup and lid designs.

Shirt Designs

These are some of my shirt designs I have done as personal projects. Most of them have been created as commissions and printed as gifts. It gives me great joy creating apparel designs due to their tangible nature.

Personal Drawings

A showcase of some of my completed works. Most of them are done in Procreate but there is also one piece done in Photoshop. I mostly draw for my own enjoyment but I have also started taking commissions recently.

Wrapping Paper Designs

A couple of wrapping paper designs I did as a personal project. It might not be customary to wrap gifts for Halloween but these paper designs might serve well for wrapping a gift for a horror fan on other occasions.

Tote Bag Design

This art piece started as a tote bag design for a birthday gift. It would look good also on a shirt and other apparel items. The color palette is limited yet vibrant and the art style is clean and cute.

Short Animation

This short animation is part of my exam project for the Adobe After Effects course I completed. Since then I’ve worked also in Adobe Animate which gave me an even further knowledge in animation.
