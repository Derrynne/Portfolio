SoGood

The goal of this project is to put together an aesthetically pleasing and user-friendly e-commerce website for a natural cosmetics business that reflects the company's values and displays its products. The target audience are individuals who are conscious of their health and seek out organic and eco-friendly cosmetics. The design portrays this through a modern and minimalistic look with natural color schemes and imagery. The website is easily navigatable and contains intuitive product categories.
